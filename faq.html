How early should I arrive at the airport? ‚ñº
We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. This allows time for check-in, security screening, and finding your gate.
Where can I find my gate information? ‚ñº
Gate information is displayed on your boarding pass and on the flight information screens throughout the airport. Gates are typically assigned 1-2 hours before departure.
What if my flight is delayed or cancelled? ‚ñº
Check the flight information screens or visit the airline counter for rebooking options. Airlines will provide accommodation and meal vouchers for significant delays when applicable.
üé´ Check-In & Boarding
Can I check in online? ‚ñº
Yes! Online check-in opens 24 hours before your flight departure. You can check in via the airline's website or mobile app, then print your boarding pass at the kiosk or show it on your mobile device.
What if I can't find my booking? ‚ñº
Double-check your booking reference (6 characters) and ensure your last name is spelled exactly as it appears on your booking. If you still can't find it, visit the airline counter for assistance.
Can I change my seat after check-in? ‚ñº
Yes, you can change your seat at the gate or through the airline's mobile app before boarding. Some seat changes may incur additional fees depending on your fare type.
üß≥ Baggage Information
What's the baggage weight limit? ‚ñº
Standard checked baggage: 23kg (50 lbs). Carry-on baggage: 8kg (17 lbs). Exceeding these limits will result in additional fees. Business and First Class passengers may have higher allowances.
Can I bring liquids in my carry-on? ‚ñº
Liquids must be in containers of 100ml or less and placed in a clear, resealable plastic bag (max 1 liter). Each passenger is limited to one bag. Larger liquids must be checked.
What items are prohibited in baggage? ‚ñº
Prohibited items include: explosives, flammable materials, compressed gases, sharp objects in carry-on, lithium batteries (loose), and toxic substances. Check with your airline for a complete list.
What if my baggage is lost or damaged? ‚ñº
Report lost or damaged baggage immediately at the airline's baggage service desk in the arrivals area. Keep your baggage claim tags as proof of ownership.
üõÇ Security & Documentation
What documents do I need for domestic flights? ‚ñº
You need a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or national ID card) and your boarding pass.
What documents do I need for international flights? ‚ñº
You need a valid passport (must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates), visa (if required for your destination), boarding pass, and any required health certificates or vaccination records.
How long does security screening take? ‚ñº
Security screening typically takes 15-30 minutes but can be longer during peak hours. We recommend arriving early to account for potential delays.

Where can I charge my devices? ‚ñº
